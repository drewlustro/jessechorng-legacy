Three-Sixty
Steel, Concrete, Skateboard

A kinetic sculpture that expresses all flips and rotations involved with every skateboard trick. I was specifically interested in dissecting the movements involved with 360 Flips.

This project was part of the Hacking Cool series, which included devices created as shortcuts for achieving childhood dreams of achieving cool.
