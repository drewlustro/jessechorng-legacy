SYNTHESHREDDER
Interactive skate bowl

The Syntheshredder is an interactive skate bowl that acts as both an instrument and performance platform. The bowl is a half sphere 18 ft in diameter with a seven foot radial transition. Embedded beneath the surface are 76 contact microphones used as sound sources and triggers.

This project was funded through Kickstarter. As rewards for backers, a 7" vinyl record was pressed as an artifact from the installation.

SYNTHESHREDDER LP

As both a capture and input device, the Syntheshredder allowed artists to create new sounds based on the movement of skateboarders. Sean Rowry used sounds from the bowl to compose two tracks.

The tracks were pressed on to 7" vinyl in an edition of 300 pieces.
